# **OSE Member Club Guide**

**Version 1.0 • Updated March 7, 2025**

## **1\. Introduction**

The OSE Member Club is a legally defined community within the Open Source Endowment Foundation designed to deepen donor engagement and provide donors the ability to influence fund distribution methods as well as organizational governance. This guide outlines the purpose, membership criteria, benefits, and operational guidelines for the Member Club.

---

## **2\. Purpose & Vision**

* **Purpose**  
  The Member Club is established to empower committed donors by providing exclusive rights, enhanced engagement opportunities, and a direct channel for influencing OSE’s distribution models and governance decisions.

* **Vision**  
  To cultivate an active community of donors who are not only financial supporters but also strategic partners in shaping the future of open source sustainability.

---

## **3\. Membership Criteria**

* A cumulative donation of **$1,000** is required to join the Member Club  
* Membership is maintained by a nominal renewal of **$10** annually

---

## **4\. Benefits & Privileges**

* Member Club members gain voting privileges on key decisions, including the selection and prioritization of distribution models  
* Starting in 2027, members will also participate in the election of at least one Community Board Member  
* Members gain access to a dedicated communications channel (e.g., Slack/Discord/email group) for direct engagement with OSE leadership, board members, and other Member Club members  
* Members receive invitations to exclusive Member Club roundtables, webinars, and virtual/in-person events  
* Members are listed in the annual OSE report and featured on the OSE website (subject to member consent)

---

## **5\. Roles & Responsibilities**

* Members are encouraged to actively participate in discussions, development, and decision making regarding distribution models, governance proposals, and organization actions and operations  
* Members are encouraged to serve as ambassadors for OSE, helping to spread awareness and encourage further engagement within the open source community  
* Members are encouraged to provide constructive feedback on OSE initiatives and suggest improvements to both operational processes and strategic directions

---

## **6\. Governance, Processes, Communication**

* The Member Club plays a consultative role in the selection of fund distribution models. While the Board retains final approval for fund distributions, the collective voice of the Member Club is essential in shaping final decisions  
* Voting is conducted online with transparent processes to ensure fairness and accountability  
* Detailed voting guidelines and schedules will be communicated ahead of each voting cycle  
* A dedicated Member Club coordinator, currently filled by the Executive Director, will oversee membership records, ensure timely renewals, and facilitate engagement activities  
* Regular updates on OSE’s performance, upcoming initiatives, strategic developments, and governance matters will be provided to members
