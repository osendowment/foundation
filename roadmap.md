When/if 501(c)(3) status is approved by the IRS (ETA ~ Q4 2025), the OSE would become a tax-exempt 501(c)(3) charity in the USA. At that point, both future donations and past contributions will retroactively qualify as tax-deductible for U.S. residents. When this milestone is reached, we plan to make this repo public, stop acquiring of co-founding donors, and facilitate donations from a broad dev community.
